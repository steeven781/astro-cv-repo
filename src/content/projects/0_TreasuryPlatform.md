Treasury Platform is an institutional-grade digital asset management system developed by AlphaPoint, designed to centralize the issuance, redemption, and lifecycle management of tokenized treasury securities and stablecoins. Built for authorized participants and institutional clients, it provides secure end-to-end workflows for BTL/Benji token operations, USDC/USDT/USDP transactions, and compliance-driven financial processes across global markets.
